FACILITIES

LIBRARY

Library The school has a well established library, which is accommodated in a spacious room, Thousands of reference books, story books, Innovative books, encyclopedias etc, are available. A full time librarian is In - charge of the library. Students can get the books from the librarian and should return within a week time. In case of missing the book the full cost of the book must be paid. Useful magazines and newspapers are kept in the reading room. Students are allowed to read them during lunch break, before and after school hours.

TRANSPORT

Transport The school operates mini buses for the convenience of the day scholars. In the morning parents should be ready with their wards in the particular stop well before in advance so that time may not be wasted. In the evening also parents should wait in the particular stop earlier to take back their children.Co-operation of the parents are requested. A nominal van fee will be charged according to the distance.

LABORATORY

Laboratory The school has well equipped science laboratories separately for Physics, Chemistry, Computer science and Biology. Students are regularly taken to the laboratories during science classes and the experiments are demonstrated to them. Students of classes VI to XII are allowed to participate In the demonstration classes so that they may get acquainted with the apparatus. Students are asked to make simple and low cost science teaching aids under the guidance of science teachers as a project method of teaching. Students are encouraged to collect specimens and rare objects related to the subject.
