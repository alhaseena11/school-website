"Nuturing Excellence at Don Bosco Matriculation Higher Secondary School"

Don Bosco Matriculation Higher Secondary School, situated in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu, is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education with a strong moral foundation. Established in 1989 and managed by the Salesians of Don Bosco, the school follows the Matriculation curriculum and offers classes from kindergarten to higher secondary levels. With a vibrant student community of over 3000, the school operates from 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM, focusing on academic excellence, character building, and extracurricular development. The institution offers a multilingual approach, providing instruction in English and Tamil, preparing students for global and local challenges.
